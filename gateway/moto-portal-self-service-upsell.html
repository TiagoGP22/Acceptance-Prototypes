MOTO

Take card payments over the phone — Mail Order / Telephone Order

Manual entry payments

Key card details directly into your terminal to accept phone and mail order payments.

Uses your existing terminals No setup or monthly cost Reviewed in 3 working days
Can you send a payment link instead?
Payment Links are the quickest and most secure way to get paid remotely. The customer pays on a secure checkout page — no card details need to be shared with you.
Learn about Payment Links ›

How it works

1
Take the order over the phone
Agree the order and total with your customer while they're on the call.
2
Key the card details straight into the terminal
On an enabled terminal in MOTO mode, type the card number, expiry and CVV as the customer reads them out.
3
Take the payment and send a receipt
Confirm the payment and send a receipt by email or SMS.

Pricing

Surcharge+1%

Applied on top of your standard acquiring fee for each manual entry payment. No setup or monthly fee. The surcharge reflects the higher fraud risk of card-not-present transactions.

Subject to approval

Because card-not-present payments carry more risk, every request goes through an eligibility check and isn't guaranteed. If approved, we'll enable MOTO on your chosen terminals — reviews are typically complete in 3 working days.

How to take a MOTO payment

  1. On an enabled terminal, choose MOTO from the main menu
  2. Enter the payment amount
  3. Key in the card number, expiry and CVV as the customer reads them out
  4. Confirm the payment and send a receipt by email or SMS

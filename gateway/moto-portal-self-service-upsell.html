MOTO

MOTO (mail order / telephone order) payments allow you to key card details directly into your terminal to accept payments taken over the phone or by mail.

Can you send a payment link instead?
Payment Links are the most secure way to get paid remotely. The customer pays on a secure checkout page — no card details need to be shared with you.

How to take a MOTO payment

  1. On your terminal, choose MOTO from the main menu
  2. Enter the payment amount
  3. Key in the card number, expiry and CVV as the customer reads them out
  4. Confirm the payment and send a receipt by email or SMS
